## Order Driver Feature

This section outlines the features and functionalities related to the order driver within the client application.

### 1. Order Assignment and Acceptance

*   **Real-time Order Notifications:** Drivers receive instant notifications for new order assignments, including order details (pickup/delivery locations, items, estimated time).
*   **Order Acceptance/Rejection:** Drivers can accept or reject an order within a specified timeframe. Rejection reasons can be provided.
*   **Automatic Reassignment:** If an order is rejected or not accepted within the time limit, it is automatically reassigned to another available driver.

### 2. Order Management and Tracking

*   **Active Orders List:** Drivers have a clear view of their currently assigned and accepted orders.
*   **Order Details View:** For each order, drivers can access comprehensive details:
    *   Customer information (name, contact number)
    *   Pickup address with navigation integration
    *   Delivery address with navigation integration
    *   List of items to be picked up/delivered
    *   Special instructions from the customer or restaurant/store
    *   Estimated pickup and delivery times
    *   Payment status (if applicable)
*   **Status Updates:** Drivers can update the order status at various stages:
    *   "En Route to Pickup"
    *   "Arrived at Pickup"
    *   "Items Picked Up"
    *   "En Route to Delivery"
    *   "Arrived at Delivery"
    *   "Order Delivered"
*   **Navigation Integration:** Seamless integration with mapping services (e.g., Google Maps, Apple Maps) for turn-by-turn directions to pickup and delivery locations.
*   **Customer Communication:** In-app chat or call functionality to communicate with the customer regarding order updates or issues.

### 3. Earnings and History

*   **Earnings Dashboard:** Drivers can view their daily, weekly, and monthly earnings.
*   **Order History:** A record of all completed orders, including details like date, time, earnings, and customer feedback.
*   **Payment Withdrawal:** Functionality to request payment withdrawals to their linked bank accounts.
